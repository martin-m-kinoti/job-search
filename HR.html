Human Resource (HR) Manager

Job Description

To oversee and implement policies and practices that help manage the organization's workforce.

Key Responsibilities

1. Managing the hiring process from job posting to onboarding, including interviewing and selecting candidates.

2. Fostering a positive work environment by addressing employee concerns, resolving conflicts, and promoting team morale.

3. Establishing and overseeing performance appraisal systems to evaluate employees’ progress and contributions.

4. Creating and managing training programs that develop employees’ skills and prepare them for career advancement.

5. Designing and managing competitive salary structures and benefits packages that attract and retain top talent.

Qualifications

1. Bachelor’s degree in human resources, business administration, psychology, or a related field.

2. SHRM Certified Professional (SHRM-CP) or Senior Certified Professional (SHRM-SCP) for strategic HR expertise.

3. Other relevant certifications like Certified Compensation Professional (CCP) or Talent Management Practitioner (TMP) can also be beneficial.

4. Generally, 5+ years of experience in HR roles, with some years in a supervisory or management position.

5. Skills in negotiation, conflict resolution, and mediation to handle employee concerns and disputes effectively.
