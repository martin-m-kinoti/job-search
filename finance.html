Finance Manager

Job Description

Ensuring the organization's financial health, strategic growth, and efficient allocation of resources.

Key Responsibilities

1. Developing budgets, forecasting future revenue and expenses, and conducting financial analysis to guide strategic decisions.

2. Preparing financial reports, such as profit and loss statements and balance sheets, to provide clear insights into financial performance.

3. Identifying areas to optimize spending and improve profitability, ensuring that resources are used efficiently.

4. Ensuring that all financial practices align with laws and regulations, such as tax obligations and auditing standards.

5. Identifying and managing financial risks, including those related to investments, currency exchange, and liquidity.

4. Typically, 5+ years of experience in financial roles, with some time in a managerial capacity.

5. Proficiency in financial software (such as SAP, Oracle, or QuickBooks) and advanced knowledge of Excel.
