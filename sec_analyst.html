Security Analyst

Job Description

Reporting to the lead Security Anlayst-the role holder will be responsible for monitoring and analyzing the company system and reporting to the lead Secutity Analyst on the findings.

Key Responsibilities

1. Continuously monitor security systems and tools (e.g., firewalls, intrusion detection systems) for any signs of breaches or attacks.

2. Regularly evaluate potential risks and prioritize areas for remediation.

3. Act as the first line of defense in the event of a security incident, taking quick action to contain and mitigate the damage, and conduct post-incident analyses to identify weaknesses and develop strategies to prevent future incidents

4. Develop, implement, and maintain security protocols, policies, and procedures in line with best practices and compliance standards.

5. Conduct penetration testing to identify vulnerabilities that could be exploited by malicious actors.
